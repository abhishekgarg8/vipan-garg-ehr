Quick clinical chronology:
- `2022-04 to 2022-06`: Back pain, high ESR, bone lesions, and diagnostic workup led to multiple myeloma diagnosis.
- `2022-06 to 2022-10`: First-line DaraVCD-based treatment produced a major biochemical response.
- `2022-12`: MRD became negative and transplant was canceled because complete remission was achieved.
- `2023-01 to 2023-08`: Maintenance bortezomib plus zoledronic acid; August 2023 MRD remained negative.
- `2023-12 to 2024-02`: Biochemical relapse emerged, then bone marrow and PET/CT confirmed recurrent disease.
- `2024-03`: CAR-T was recommended because he was not considered ASCT eligible; no record found so far confirms CAR-T was administered.
- `2024-04 to 2024-09-04`: Post-relapse therapy is now documented as 6 cycles of carfilzomib + daratumumab + zoledronic acid + pomalidomide, ending 04 Sep 2024.
- `2024-09`: Major renal and systemic hospitalization with AKI/anuria, dialysis context, anemia, thrombocytopenia, and pleural effusions. Later kidney biopsy described thrombotic microangiopathy and noted post-carfilzomib-induced TMA.
- `2025-01 to 2025-08`: Maintenance daratumumab + pomalidomide is documented; on 21 Aug 2025 he received Cycle 20 Day 1 maintenance daratumumab plus denosumab.
- `2024-11 to 2026-04`: Persistent low-level IgG-kappa monoclonal disease with chronic kidney disease and cytopenias, followed by a stronger April 2026 kappa rise.

Caveats:
- Some dates are month-level because the available source was a summary or a merged report rather than a single dated lab sheet.
- This is a research and organization archive, not medical advice.
