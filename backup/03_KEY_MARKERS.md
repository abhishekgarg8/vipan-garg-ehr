## Most important pattern

- The disease responded extremely well in 2022 and reached MRD-negative remission.
- Remission was still documented in August 2023.
- Biochemical relapse emerged in December 2023 and was confirmed structurally in February 2024.
- The prior treatment gap is now partly filled: post-relapse treatment was a carfilzomib/daratumumab/pomalidomide/zoledronic-acid regimen until 04 September 2024, followed by severe AKI and biopsy-described post-carfilzomib TMA.
- Later records from late 2024 through 2026 show persistent IgG-kappa monoclonal disease with chronic kidney dysfunction rather than a durable clean second remission, despite a February 2025 marrow MRD-nil checkpoint.
