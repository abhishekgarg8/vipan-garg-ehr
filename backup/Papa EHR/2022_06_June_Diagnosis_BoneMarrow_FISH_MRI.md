## Confirmatory Tests Performed

### 1. Bone Marrow Biopsy
- **Bone Marrow / Serum Protein Electrophoresis** performed in June 2022.

### 2. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) Report

**ABNORMAL findings:**
- **3–4 copies each** of **1q21 (CKS1B locus)** and **1p32 (CDKN2C locus)** seen in **10% of cells**.
- Suggestive of **trisomy/tetrasomy of chromosome 1** — associated with adverse prognosis in MM.

**NORMAL/NEGATIVE findings (no evidence of):**
- 1p32 (CDKN2C) deletion
- t(4;14)(p16;q32) FGFR3::IGH translocation
- t(14;16)(q32;q23) IGH::MAF translocation
- t(14;20)(q32;q12) IGH::MAFB translocation
- 11q13 (CCND1) rearrangement
- 17p13 (TP53) deletion ← **important: no TP53 deletion = relatively favorable**

> **Note:** FISH performed on a **non-sorted sample** — this is clinically significant as non-sorted samples may underestimate plasma cell percentages.

> **Clinical Implication:** Gain of 1q21 is considered a **high-risk cytogenetic feature** in Multiple Myeloma, associated with shorter progression-free survival and potentially poorer outcomes.

### 3. MRI of Spine

- **D1 and D6 vertebrae have collapsed** (compression fractures).
- Bone lesions found in spine and some ribs — consistent with MM bone disease.
- CARB classification: **C-ve (calcium normal), A-ve (anemia mild), R-ve (renal normal), B+ve (bone lesions present)**
