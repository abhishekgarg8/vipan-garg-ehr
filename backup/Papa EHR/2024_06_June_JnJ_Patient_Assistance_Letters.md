# June 2024 — Johnson & Johnson Patient Assistance Program for Darzalex

**Date:** June 1–4, 2024
**Phase:** Second-line Treatment (Post-Relapse)
**Patient:** Vipan Kumar Garg

---

## Context

Following the relapse in April 2024, the patient was restarted on **monthly Darzalex (daratumumab)** infusions at Max Healthcare. By June 2024, the patient had received **3 doses** post-relapse. Two formal letters were drafted to request a **complimentary 4th dose** under J&J's 3+1 Patient Assistance Program.

Later Drive review clarifies that Darzalex was one component of the broader post-relapse regimen. A Max discharge summary dated 21 Aug 2025 states that the patient received **6 cycles of carfilzomib + daratumumab + zoledronic acid + pomalidomide until 04 Sep 2024**. These June letters prove the daratumumab assistance component, not the entire regimen.
