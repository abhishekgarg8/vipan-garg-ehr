# June–October 2022 — DaraVCD Chemotherapy (5 Cycles)

**Date:** June 20, 2022 – October 2022
**Phase:** First-Line Induction Chemotherapy
**Patient:** Vipan Kumar Garg

---

## Treatment Regimen: DaraVCD

The patient underwent **5 cycles** of the **DaraVCD protocol**:

| Drug | Full Name | Dose / Schedule |
|---|---|---|
| **Dara** | Daratumumab (Darzalex) | 400mg × 3 (initial loading), then maintenance infusions |
| **V** | Bortezomib | Weekly subcutaneous injection |
| **C** | Cyclophosphamide | Weekly dose |
| **D** | Dexamethasone | 40mg weekly |

### Total Treatments Administered
- **12 Daratumumab (Darzalex) infusions** total
  - 2 doses were complimentary (Johnson & Johnson Patient Assistance Program)
  - 10 doses were paid
- **16 weekly Bortezomib injections**
- **Weekly Zoledronic acid** (bone-protective agent) for the **first 4 months**

### Drug Substitution — Lenalidomide → Cyclophosphamide
- Patient was **initially started on Lenalidomide** (standard partner drug).
- Lenalidomide was **replaced with Cyclophosphamide** because:
  - Platelet count was declining after 4 weeks of Lenalidomide.
  - Cyclophosphamide was better tolerated hematologically.

---

## Treatment Response Monitoring — July 2022 (mid-treatment)

| Test | July 2022 | Baseline (May 2022) | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Serum Kappa FLC** | **16.97 mg/L** | 857 mg/L | ↓↓↓ Dramatic reduction |
| **Serum Lambda FLC** | 11.7 mg/L | 17.8 mg/L | Slight decrease |
| **Kappa/Lambda Ratio** | **1.45** | 48.3 | Near-normal |
| **M Spike** | **0.22 g/dL** | 2.1 g/dL | ↓↓↓ Major response |
| **Gamma Globulin** | 0.512 g/dL | 2.4 g/dL | ↓↓↓ |
| **Total Protein** | 5.8 g/dL | 8.4 g/dL | Decreased (expected) |
| **Albumin** | 1.949 g/dL | 4.1 g/dL | Dropped (treatment effect) |

> **Assessment:** Rapid and dramatic response to DaraVCD after ~4 cycles. Kappa FLC dropped from 857 to 16.97 — a **>98% reduction**. M Spike nearly eliminated. This is a **very good partial response (VGPR) or better**.

---

## Zoledronic Acid (Bone Treatment)
- Zoledronic acid given **weekly for first 4 months** (June–September 2022).
- Purpose: Reduce bone loss, protect against skeletal-related events in the context of existing vertebral fractures (D1, D6).
- Subsequently changed to **quarterly dosing**.

---

## Side Effects / Complications Noted
- Platelet count suppression with Lenalidomide → drug switched.
- Albumin dropped significantly during treatment (likely due to treatment-related protein shifts).
