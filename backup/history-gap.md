## Key Corrections to Existing Summary

The earlier phrase **"treatment history after March 2024 remains incompletely documented"** should no longer be used without qualification. The gap is now partly filled:

- The post-relapse treatment was not just monthly daratumumab.
- The documented 2024 regimen included **carfilzomib, daratumumab, zoledronic acid, and pomalidomide** for 6 cycles until 04 Sep 2024.
- The September 2024 renal crash followed that regimen.
- The kidney biopsy provides a treatment-linked explanation: **post-carfilzomib-induced thrombotic microangiopathy**.
- The 2025 maintenance regimen is documented as **daratumumab + pomalidomide**.
- February 2025 marrow MRD was nil, but serum IgG-kappa disease markers persisted.

## Practical Interpretation

The patient history should now be read as:

1. Deep first remission after 2022 DaraVCD.
2. Biochemical relapse late 2023 and structural/marrow relapse in February 2024.
3. CAR-T recommended in March 2024 but not documented as administered.
4. Salvage treatment with carfilzomib + daratumumab + pomalidomide + zoledronic acid through 04 Sep 2024.
5. Severe September 2024 renal crisis later supported by biopsy as post-carfilzomib TMA.
6. Maintenance daratumumab + pomalidomide from January 2025.
7. Persistent low-level serum disease in 2025 despite Feb 2025 marrow MRD nil.
8. Renewed biochemical/MRD activity by April 2026.
