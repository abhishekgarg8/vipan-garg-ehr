## Why this report matters

This report captures the early response after treatment started on 20 June 2022.

## Treatment context

- Regimen in history summary: DaraVCD-based therapy
- Daratumumab, bortezomib, dexamethasone, and cyclophosphamide were used
- Lenalidomide was reportedly stopped after platelet decline

## Key biochemical response values

- Serum kappa free light chain: `16.97 mg/L`
- Serum lambda free light chain: `11.73 mg/L`
- Kappa/lambda ratio: `1.45`
- M-spike: `0.22 g/dL`
- Total protein: `5.8 g/dL`
- Gamma globulin: `0.512 g/dL`

## Other values mentioned in the report set

- Hemoglobin around `12.0 g/dL`
- Platelets around `110 x10^3/uL`
- Sodium around `131 mEq/L`

## Plain-English interpretation

Compared with the June 2022 diagnostic burden, these results show a major early biochemical response. The monoclonal load dropped sharply and the free light chain ratio normalized. The main caution visible in this phase is treatment-related marrow suppression, especially thrombocytopenia.
