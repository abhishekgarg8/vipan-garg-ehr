## Plain-English interpretation

This panel shows persistent IgG-kappa monoclonal disease with abnormal free light chain ratio and chronic kidney dysfunction. The beta-2 microglobulin is high, but kidney impairment may contribute to that value, so it should not be read in isolation. The merged PGIMER material adds that, despite the serum findings, the February 2025 marrow MRD test was nil.
